About The Division

>_ WHO WE ARE:
Aegis SRM is a premier student-run organization at SRM Institute of Science and Technology. Like the mythological shield it is named after, we aim to be the guardians of digital literacy and cybersecurity awareness on campus.

>_ MISSION DIRECTIVE:
We bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application. Through hands-on workshops, competitive CTFs, and expert seminars, we empower students to navigate and secure the digital frontier.

>_ DOMAINS:

  • Cybersecurity & Forensics
  • Ethical Hacking
  • Secure Development
  • Networking & IoT
